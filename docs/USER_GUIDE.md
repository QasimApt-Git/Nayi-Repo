# COSY AIRCONDITIONERS — User Guide
## How to Use the Website

---

### 1. Opening the Website
Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).

---

### 2. Navigation
The top navigation bar has these links:
- **Home** — Goes to the top hero section
- **Brands** — Shows all available brands
- **Products** — Shows the product catalog
- **Compare** — Compare two products side by side
- **Contact** — Send a message or view location

---

### 3. Browsing by Brand
1. Scroll to the **"Top Brands"** section
2. Click any **brand button** (e.g., Samsung, LG, Daikin) at the top
3. Only products of that brand will appear in the Products section
4. Click **"All Brands"** to see everything again
5. You can also click a **brand logo card** — it will filter products AND scroll you down automatically

---

### 4. Viewing Products
- Each product card shows:
  - Brand name tag (top left)
  - Capacity, Energy Rating, Technology
  - Short description
  - Price
- Click the **"Details"** button on any product to open a full specification page in a new tab

---

### 5. Comparing Products
1. Scroll to the **"Compare Products"** section
2. Select **Product A** from the first dropdown
3. Select **Product B** from the second dropdown
4. Click the **"Compare"** button
5. A detailed comparison table will appear below showing all specs side by side

---

### 6. Contact Form
1. Scroll to the **"Contact Us"** section
2. Enter your Name, Email, select inquiry type, and write your message
3. Click **"Send Message"**
4. A green confirmation message will appear

You can also email directly: **info@cosyac.com**

---

### 7. Product Detail Documents
- Each product has a **"Details"** button
- Clicking it opens an HTML document with full technical specifications
- These documents open in a new browser tab
- You can download or print them from the browser

---

### Tips
- The website works on mobile phones and tablets
- Use the scroll bar or click navigation links to jump to sections
- The AC unit animation on the home page is just decorative
